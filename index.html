Features

Roki si designed to be simple to use but comes fully featured.

No Setup, No Config

Roki is single executable file with no dependencies. It comes preconfigured with the best default and security.

Analytics

Analyse your audience right out of the box. Follow realtime metrics in your dashboard: pageviews, visitors, forms conversion rate, utms and export or share reports.

Forms

Forms working instantly.
Roki carefully collects every form submission in your dashboard where you can view, manage, and export the data.

Security

HTTPS and security by default.
Roki obtains and renews TLS certificates for your sites automatically. It even staples OCSP responses.

Convenience

Roki automatically do a lot of things for you, such as optimize your website for accessibility, SEO and performance.

Runs everywhere

Any OS, any CPU architecture. Roki take care of itself and your data by controlling its footprint.

Optimization and Convenience

Roki is a fire and forget server, it takes care of itself as of your website. It aims to do all the hassle for you.

  • Automatic security on the edge (HTTPS).
  • Handle millions of requests with one server.
  • Monitor errors (specially 404).
  • Easily manage redirections without ops.
  • Generates sitemap.
  • Compress your assets (image, html, css, js).
  • Automatic assets cache headers.
  • Aggregate and rotate the data to never running out of space.
  • Clean the output: comment from the html, exif data from images.
  • Share website from your machine: integrates perfectly with ngrok.

Ownership and Privacy

Roki is a single binary that works on any OS or platform.
The choice is yours where you host it, on metal with a kernel or in any hosting or cloud company that meet your requirements.
Roki server integrates mechanisms to meet the European GDPR compliance.
All the data collected and generated are written beside the server, you have complete control over it.
